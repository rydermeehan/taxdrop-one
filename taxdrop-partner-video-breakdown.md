## PRODUCTION NOTES

### Visual Style Guide
- **Color Palette:** TaxDrop brand (Deep Emerald #0C593E, Mint #DFFFEA, Yellow-Green Pop #C4FF64)
- **Typography:** Space Grotesk (headlines), Inter (body text)
- **Mood:** Professional, trustworthy, modern
- **Lighting:** Warm, approachable, well-lit

### B-Roll Shot List
1. Property tax documents/statements
2. Calculator and financial papers
3. Property manager at desk
4. TaxDrop dashboard/interface
5. Handshake moments
6. Houses/properties exterior
7. Happy clients reviewing savings

### Motion Graphics Needed
1. Dollar amount animations ($20, portfolio calculations)
2. Statistics counters (30-60%, 80-90%, 10-15%)
3. 3-step process icons
4. Map of Texas and California
5. Logo animations and end card

### Audio Requirements
- Professional voice (host or VO artist)
- Subtle background music (upbeat but not distracting)
- Clean audio capture, minimal room echo

### Equipment Recommendations
- 4K camera minimum
- Lavalier + shotgun mic combo
- 3-point lighting setup
- Teleprompter for clean delivery
